06 Interactive

Interactives refer to dynamic elements or components integrated into websites, applications, or digital platforms that enable users to actively engage with content, thereby enhancing user experience and interactivity. These interactive elements are designed to encourage user participation, facilitate navigation, and provide meaningful interactions with the underlying content.
