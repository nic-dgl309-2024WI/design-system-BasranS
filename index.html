Little Diamond

The "Little Diamond" shrub is a compact, low-growing evergreen plant with small, diamond-shaped leaves. It is often used as a ground cover or in landscaping for its dense foliage and neat appearance.

Leyland

"Leyland" likely refers to the Leyland Cypress, a popular evergreen tree commonly used in landscaping and Christmas tree production. This tree is a hybrid of two different cypress species and is known for its rapid growth.

Sekkon Sugi

"Sekkon Sugi" refers to a variety of Cryptomeria japonica, also known as Japanese cedar or Japanese sugi. Sekkon Sugi is prized for its distinctive foliage, which features golden-yellow tips.

Camellia

Camellia is a genus of flowering plants in the family Theaceae, native to East Asia. The most well-known species is Camellia sinensis, which is cultivated for its leaves, which are used to produce tea.

Our Nursery Tale

Outback Nursery & Garden Centre, located in Courtenay, BC, is a premier destination for gardening enthusiasts on Vancouver Island. Established in 1998-1999, our family-owned nursery spans across a spacious 6-acre property, featuring five expansive greenhouses totaling nearly 23,000 square feet. Our journey began with clearing trees and embracing country living, evolving into a thriving hub for plants, livestock, and top-quality products showcased at local markets and beyond.

After planting seeds, it's essential to provide consistent care to support their germination and growth. Water the planted seeds gently, keeping the soil moist but not waterlogged. Place the planted seeds in an appropriate location with the right amount of ... sunlight as indicated on the seed packet. Monitor the soil moisture regularly and water as needed to prevent drying out. Be patient and observant, as germination times may vary depending on the type of seed and environmental conditions. Keep an eye out for any signs of pests or diseases and take appropriate measures to protect the seeds. With proper care and attention, your planted seeds will have the best chance of developing into healthy, thriving plants.
