12 Footer

A website footer is the section located at the bottom of a webpage. It typically contains important information such as copyright details, contact information, navigation links, social media links, and sometimes a site map or subscribe form. The footer provides users with additional resources and enhances website navigation by offering easy access to important pages and information.
